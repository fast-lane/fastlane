Frequently Asked Questions

What are typical project timelines?
Most proof-of-concepts complete in 10-15 days. Full implementations typically run 2-6 weeks depending on complexity. We focus on rapid delivery of working solutions rather than lengthy planning phases.
How do you handle data security?
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We can work with on-premise deployments, private cloud, or air-gapped environments. We're ISO 27001 compliant and follow GDPR requirements by default. Client data is never used to train models without explicit written permission.
On-premise vs cloud deployment?
We support both. Cloud deployments offer faster time-to-value and easier scaling. On-premise gives you complete control and may be required for regulatory reasons. We'll help you choose based on your security, compliance, and performance needs.
What's your pricing model?
Proof-of-concepts are fixed-fee (typically ‚Ç¨15-25k). Full projects can be fixed-fee or time-and-materials based on your preference. We provide clear estimates upfront and don't charge for initial consultations. No hidden costs or surprise overruns.
How do you measure success?
Every project starts with defined, measurable success criteria. Common metrics include processing time reduction, error rate improvement, cost savings, and user adoption rates. We provide regular reporting against these metrics and adjust our approach based on results.
